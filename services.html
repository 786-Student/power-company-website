Our Services

PowerCo offers a range of cost-saving solutions tailored to the needs of utility companies. These services span from strategic product sourcing to comprehensive warehouse and material management. By leveraging its buying power, supply chain expertise, and advanced technology platforms, PowerCo helps utility partners reduce overhead, streamline operations, and improve overall efficiency.

Key Benefits of PowerCo’s Material Management Solutions

  • Reduction in internal personnel and overhead
  • Increased inventory turnover
  • Lower inventory carrying costs
  • Electronic order and invoicing systems
  • Job kitting and jobsite deliveries
  • Emergency preparedness action plans
  • Full access to PowerCo’s extensive inventory

With one of the largest regional inventories in the industry, PowerCo is also a proud member of the North American Association of Utility Distributors (NAAUD), offering customers access to over $400 million in inventory through a network of trusted NAAUD partners.

Additional Services PowerCo Provides to Utilities

  • Inventory Management Solutions
  • Custom Packaging Solutions
  • 24/7 Emergency Response Services
  • System Integration and Automation Solutions
