About Us

Our Mission

At Grid Solved, we specialize in the supply and strategic procurement of high-voltage and low-voltage materials for utility, industrial, and infrastructure projects. Our mission is to deliver reliable, schedule-aligned solutions that meet the demands of critical power systems and construction timelines.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of high-quality materials — including transformers, HV/LV breakers, capacitor banks, instrument transformers, switches, steel, transmission line materials, and insulators — all sourced from trusted global manufacturers.

But we go far beyond supply. Our team works closely with clients to identify long lead-time materials, coordinate with qualified vendors, and manage procurement with transparency and precision.

End-to-End Support

From single orders to large-scale project support, we offer real-time tracking, clear communication, and dedicated service from inquiry to delivery. Our project managers help align material logistics with site activities — reducing delays and improving efficiency.

Cybersecurity in Energy

In today’s increasingly connected environment, we support our partners with grid-focused cybersecurity services to protect critical infrastructure and digital assets.

Built on Three Core Principles:

Reliable materials. Responsive service. Results delivered on schedule.
