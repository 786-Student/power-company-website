About Us

At Grid Solved, we specialize in the supply and strategic procurement of high-voltage and low-voltage materials for utility, industrial, and infrastructure projects. Our mission is to deliver reliable, schedule-aligned solutions that meet the demands of critical power systems and construction timelines.

We offer a wide range of high-quality materials — including transformers, HV/LV breakers, capacitor banks, instrument transformers, switches, steel, transmission line materials, and insulators — sourced from trusted global manufacturers. But we go far beyond supply.

Our team works closely with clients to identify long lead-time materials, coordinate with qualified vendors, and manage procurement processes with transparency and precision. From single orders to large-scale project support, we provide clear communication, real-time tracking, and dedicated service from inquiry to delivery.

We also offer project management services for substation and transmission developments, helping clients align material logistics with site activities. In today’s increasingly connected environment, we further support our partners with grid-focused cybersecurity services to protect critical infrastructure.

At Grid Solved, we are built on three core principles:
Reliable materials. Responsive service. Results delivered on schedule.
