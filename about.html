About Grid Solved

Delivering Reliability, Quality, and Precision Since Day One

Our Mission

At Grid Solved, we specialize in the supply and strategic procurement of high- and low-voltage materials for utility, industrial, and infrastructure projects. Our mission: deliver reliable, schedule-aligned solutions that empower critical systems and timelines.

What We Offer

We supply high-quality materials—transformers, breakers, capacitor banks, switches, steel, line components, and insulators—from globally trusted manufacturers.

Beyond hardware, our team helps manage long lead times, vendor coordination, and transparent procurement processes.

End-to-End Support

From small orders to large-scale projects, we provide real-time tracking, communication, and personalized service. Our project managers align logistics with site schedules to reduce delays.

Cybersecurity in Energy

In today’s connected world, we support partners with grid-focused cybersecurity services to safeguard critical infrastructure and digital systems.

Built on Three Core Principles:

Reliable materials. Responsive service. Results on schedule.
